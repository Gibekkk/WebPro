Arctic Knight

The frozen knight of Warredion

About Arctic Knight:

Passive
Frostbite

Your skills have a 20% chance to apply frostbite to hit enemies. Enemies with the frostbite effect take damage overtime.

Basic Attack
Frost Strike

Strike enemies with your frigid lance. Every third strike is a downslam strike that creates a small ice crater, setting enemies slightly airborne.

Skill 1
Glacial Impalement

Summon your frigid lance and take aim. After 3 seconds, your frigid lance fires forwards with high speed, piercing through all enemies.

Skill 2
Glacier Smash

Leap upwards and smash down to the surface, creating a big ice crater. Enemies in the crater area gets completely frozen.

Skill 3
Arctic Charge

Focus your glacial energy and charge forwards with the spirit of the arctic wolf. Hit enemies get thrown backwards. You also leave behind a path of ice that slows and damages enemies.

Skill 4
Frozen Shield

Summon your frozen shield, disallowing enemies to approach you and blocking projectiles. After a few seconds you push your frozen shield forwards, knocking back enemies.

Ultimate
Permafrost Lance

Summon your permafrost lance, slash through your enemies 2 times, knocking them back. You knock up your enemies with your 3rd strike, setting them airborne. Your last strike completely pins enemies to the surface, freezing them for a long time.
