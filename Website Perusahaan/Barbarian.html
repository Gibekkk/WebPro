Barbarian

The bloodthirsty hunter of Warredion

About Barbarian:

Passive
Bloodlust

You have a 5% chance to heal yourself whenever you strike an enemy.

Basic Attack
Rageblade

Strike an enemy with your axe, with a 10% chance to cause bleeding.

Skill 1
Primal Axe

Hurl an axe forwards, striking all enemies in contact. The axe gets pinned when it gets in contact with a block. Picking it up instantly resets your Primal Axe skill cooldown.

Skill 2
War Cry

Bellow a mighty war cry, increasing resistance and strength, taunting nearby enemies to be pulled in towards you.

Skill 3
Relentless Leap

Leap forward and smash down the surface with your axe, creating a cone rupture that instantly pulls in nearby enemies.

Skill 4
Double Edge

Release a wide double slash, damaging and causing bleeding to hit enemies. This powerful move gives you the same amount of damage and bleeding.

Ultimate
Eternal Fury

Release your full internal rage, gain resistance, speed, and regeneration. Your Rageblade skill becomes stronger, wider, and bleeding chance increases to 50%.
