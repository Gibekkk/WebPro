Illusionist

The trickster sorcerer of Warredion

About Illusionist:

Passive
Queen Tarot

Cards orbit around you, stunning enemies that attack you when the cards are active.

Basic Attack
Card Throw

Throw 1~3 cards at your enemies.

Skill 1
Phantom Blink

Teleport diagonally backwards throwing 3 cards. Your mirror clone dashes through your path again, damaging enemies in contact with your clone.

Skill 2
Mirror Shuriken

Toss a mirror shuriken that knocks back enemies. Your mirror shuriken comes back to you, knocking back enemies in the opposite direction this time. Damaged enemies are left with a bleeding effect.

Skill 3
Mirage Slot

Teleport forwards. Your mirror clone dashes through your path again, damaging enemies in contact with your clone.

Skill 4
Mirror Needles

Summon 2 mirrors on your left and right side. Each mirror fires 3 mirror needles in order.

Ultimate
Mirror Dimension

Summon a mirror portal beneath you while stunning nearby enemies inside your mirror portal. After some time, you teleport your mirror portal to the direction you are looking at. Stunned enemies inside the mirror portal are teleported as well.
